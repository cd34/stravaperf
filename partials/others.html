Other sites that use the Strava API

  • StravaFriends - find other people in your area that have ridden segments that you've ridden.
  • VeloViewer - a dashboard for viewing your Strava Data.
  • RaceShape - see your performance against others on segments you've ridden to take back that KOM.
  • Segment Details - analysis of segment KOMs.
  • Strava Heatmaps - See where you ride most frequently.
